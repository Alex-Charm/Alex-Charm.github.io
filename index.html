The Beauty of France

France is a country in Western Europe, known for its medieval cities, alpine villages, and Mediterranean beaches.

National Anthem: La Marseillaise

Bonus: Football Legends

France has one of the most successful football teams in history, known as Les Bleus.

  • Zinedine Zidane: A midfield maestro and iconic leader of the 1998 World Cup win.
  • Thierry Henry: A legendary striker and Arsenal's all-time top scorer.
  • Kylian Mbappé: The modern superstar leading the new generation of French football.

Did you know?

France is the most visited country in the world, receiving nearly 90 million visitors annually!
